Preface

Humans have always evolved and tried to simplify their lives, and for this they had to invent tools that would make their work easier.

Transportation

A clear example of technology development is transportation. A clear example of technology development is transportation. If in ancient times people traveled on foot or on horseback and it took a lot of time to get from one point to another, now there are many means of transportation that allow us to move in much less time. For example: planes, ships, cars, train, etc.

Communications

The development of technology has facilitated the formation of simple communications. Nowadays, we no longer need to wait for months to receive a letter, because we can contact each other instantly through social networks.

Medicine

Medicine is one of the main things that technology has contributed to. If it weren't for modern medical devices, we still wouldn't be able to treat many diseases.

Finally we can safely say that technology has made our life much easier.
